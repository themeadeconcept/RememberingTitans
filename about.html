About Our Organization

Thank you for visiting this page. Unfortunately, I can't license anything on this page, nor am I a journalist. This is simply a page to show my work as Professional Web Developer. I am a HUGE sports fan. So creation of this page did not feel like a chore. If you would like to know a little about me, first and foremost...I stand by my San Francisco 49ers and Chicago Bulls. Secondly, I am a freelance Web Developer with a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology. I have been coding for a little less than a year and since the pandemic happened, I have been EXTREMELY involved with tech. I have a beautiful wife, two dogs, and 3 gorgeous children.

As for creation of this website. I will gladly share the Git Repository with you if you would like to check it out! Technologies that I included in this project were Pseudo-selectors, grid, and flex-box. This was project was not created with any frameworks, strictly PURE HTML/CSS. Thanks for visiting and if you would like to connect, feel free to click my social media icons up top or send me an email using the Form on the bottom. Thanks again!
